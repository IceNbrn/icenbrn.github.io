HOTSHOTS

Hotshots is a game where you play as the commander of a firehouse. You can buy new vehicles as well as recruit firefighters to aid in your mission.

This game was made by me and a group of friends, we even have a name for it, TMWStudios.
To make this game we were inspired by a friend that is a firefighter, so we made HotShots.
The player plays as the commander of a firehouse, where he needs to manage, buy, and even helping on the field.

The game right now has 3 vehicles

Each vehicle has a price and depending on its function it can be more expensive.

The main objective of the game is to put out fires. When you complete a mission, you will receive a reward.

So one of the features that I made, water system. Gives the ability to fill up the vehicles water tank or even transfer to other vehicle.

To use it, there's a hose, the player needs to attach to a vehicle and to a water source, that can be a hydrant or a vehicle

With this, the truck VUCI will start filling up
