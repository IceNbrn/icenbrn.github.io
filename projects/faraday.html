FARADAY

.

Faraday was a project made in 48hours for IPCA GameJam, and it was created by TMStudios, and I was part of it.

We had a theme(light), so why not use the sun light? That's where the idea comes from.
This is a manager, where the player needs to manage a eletric station to keep the cars running. To produce electricity you need solar panels, then you need batteries to store the power in excess and finnaly a eletrical box to connect the solar panels and the batteries.

The player gets money when a car charges and with that money the player can expand the station, with more slots to charge, more batteries and solar panels.

I worked on the User Interface and the Shop System.
